ReflectRally helps engineering teams manage **Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)**. Instead of static markdown files, decisions become structured, reviewable, and easy to discover.

## Problems ReflectRally Solves

### Nobody wrote down the "why"
Decisions happen in meetings, Slack threads, or pull requests—then disappear. When you need the reasoning six months later, it's gone.

### No clear ownership of decisions
Architecture choices happen informally, without clear ownership. When problems come up, nobody knows who decided what—or what trade-offs were considered.

### New engineers lack context
Without written decision records, onboarding slows down. New team members inherit complex systems but can't trace why anything was built that way.

## Workflow

1. **Create a decision draft** - Write down the context, assumptions, alternatives, and trade-offs.
2. **Review with your team** - Share the decision with teammates for feedback.
3. **Accept & lock it in** - Accepted decisions become immutable. If things change later, you supersede the decision.
4. **Revisit when things change** - Set up scheduled reviews or connect decisions to signals.

## Key Features

- **Immutable decision records** - Accepted decisions can't be silently changed. Full tamper-proof history.
- **Linked assumptions & dependencies** - Connect decisions to their assumptions and affected systems.
- **Attention alerts** - Get notified when assumptions break, dependencies change, or a decision needs review.
- **Team review workflow** - Discuss decisions with clear ownership and async collaboration.
- **Search & filter decisions** - Find any decision instantly. Filter by status, risk, or attention needs.
- **Role-based access control** - Separate contributors from viewers.
